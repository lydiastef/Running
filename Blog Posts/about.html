About Me

Welcome to my running website!

Hey there, I'm Lydia and I'm a passionate runner who loves marathon and ultra-marathon distances. Running brings me so much joy, whether I'm hitting the road, exploring beautiful trails, or running in the mountains! As a dedicated runner, I totally get the ups and downs, the victories, and the pure excitement that comes from pushing our boundaries. Right now, I'm working on becoming a certified running coach so I can share my experiences and knowledge with others. On this platform, I want to offer you valuable insights, training tips, and all sorts of helpful information to supercharge your running journey. Let's come together and embrace the incredible transformation that running can bring to our lives! Join me on this adventure, and let's make running an extraordinary part of our everyday!
