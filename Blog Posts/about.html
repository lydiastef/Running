About Me

Welcome to my running website!

My name is Lydia and I am an avid runner with a deep passion for marathon and ultra marathon distances. I find joy in every run, whether it's hitting the road, exploring the trails, or conquering mountains.
As a dedicated runner myself, I understand the challenges, triumphs, and the sheer exhilaration that comes with pushing our limits.
Currently, I'm pursuing certification as a running coach to share my knowledge and experience with others.
On this platform, I aim to provide valuable insights, training tips, and useful information to help you enhance your running journey. Join me as we embrace the transformative power of running together.
