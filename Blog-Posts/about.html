About Me

Welcome to my running website!

Hey there, I'm Lydia and I'm a passionate runner who loves marathon and ultra-marathon distances. Running is like meditation for me, it calms down my mind and my nerves. I like road and trail running equally and doing both brings variety and more fun to running.
As a dedicated runner, I totally get the ups and downs, the victories, and the pure excitement that comes from pushing our boundaries.
Right now, I'm working on becoming a certified running coach so I can help others find the joy in running and share my experiences and knowledge.
On this platform, I want to offer you valuable insights, training tips, and all sorts of helpful information to supercharge your running journey. Join me on this adventure, and let's make running an extraordinary part of our everyday!
